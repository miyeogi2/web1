할리스 커피

Hollys Coffee (Korean: 할리스 커피) is a specialty coffee company headquartered in South Korea. The company was established in 1998 and opened Korea's first espresso shop that year. Its shops offer espresso, coffee, tea, specialty coffee- and tea-based hot and cold drinks, a variety of pastries and other snacks and coffee supplies.

By 2008 there were more than 100 Hollys Coffee shops in Korea, and the franchise had expanded with additional stores in China, Thailand, Vietnam and Malaysia. It has a barista academy called the Hollys Coffee Academy.
